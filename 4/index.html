Ernest Hemingway also was a lover of cats and kittens. He now has a specific type of cat named after him and his cats he used to take care of. They are called Hemingway Cats. Do you know why? Because they have six toes. These are also called polydactyl cats and sailors believed they were good luck. Their extra toes made them better mousers and helped them balance on the high seas. Many of his cats still live in his house in Key West and it is believed that the cats in Key West are all related to each other because of this.
